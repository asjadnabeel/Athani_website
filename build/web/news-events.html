ATHANI CAMPUS INAUGURATED

Dr. M.K. Muneer, Honorable Minister for Panchayat and Social Welfare inaugurated "ATHANI CAMPUS" on Friday morning at Narikkuni in a function presided over by Sri. V.M. Ummer Master MLA.

The campus is home to the ATHANI Homecare and Outpatient Clinics, ATHANI Destitute Home under construction, proposed ATHANI Rehabilitation and Occupational Training Centre, proposed Physiotherapy centre, and proposed Diabetic/Kidney care clinic. Dr. Muneer lauded the efforts of ATHANI towards the welfare of unfortunate victims of serious diseases, and urged the team to go ahead with the work in the same intensity and vigour.

A number of dignitaries including Jb.CP Kunhi Mohammed, Jb.Karat Razak Master, and Sri. IP Rajesh attended the event. A cultural program was held at the campus in the evening to mark its inauguration. Sri. Faisal Elettil presided over the event. The performances were led by famous singer Master Badusha.
