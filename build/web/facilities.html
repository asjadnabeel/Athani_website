Physiotherapy Centre

Run by ATHANI (registered under societies act registration NO:). Physiotherapy also known as Physical therapy is a health care profession, which views human movement as central to the health and well being of individuals. It is a holistic approach to healthcare, and plays a vital part in pain relief, healing and rehabilitation in most medical and surgical conditions. The In-patient Physiotherapy process consists of

  • Assessment

  • Treatment-appropriate pain reliving Modality

  • Exercises (Isometrics, strengthening, stretching)

  • Home program and advice

  • Counseling

  • Getting informed consent
